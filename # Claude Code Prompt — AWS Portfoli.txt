### Design requirements

- **Style:** Clean, minimal, professional — think modern engineer portfolio. Lots of whitespace. Sans-serif body text. Subtle hover transitions on cards and buttons.
- **Color palette:** Mostly neutral (white background, dark gray/near-black text), with one accent color. Use a desaturated AWS-adjacent navy or teal (e.g. `#0F4C75` or `#0F766E`) for accents — NOT the loud AWS orange.
- **Typography:** Inter for body, weight 400 and 500. Section headings can be slightly larger/bolder. Don't go above font-weight 600.
- **No:** gradients, drop shadows beyond the most subtle, glassmorphism, animated backgrounds, autoplay videos, popups, or anything cute.
- **Yes:** functional focus rings on links/buttons (accessibility), `prefers-reduced-motion` respected, semantic HTML5 (`<header>`, `<main>`, `<section>`, `<footer>`), descriptive alt text on all images.
